# Log

Performs the natural logarithm on the input parameter.


$$
A = \begin{bmatrix}
    -1 & 2          \\\\
    3 & -4
\end{bmatrix}, 
B = \begin{bmatrix}
    2 & 4          \\\\
    6 & 8
\end{bmatrix}, 
C = \begin{bmatrix}
    -1 \\\\
    3
\end{bmatrix},
D = \begin{bmatrix}
    -1 & 3
\end{bmatrix}

$$

### Log(Matrix) = Matrix


$$
Log(A) = \begin{bmatrix}
    NaN & 0.6931472 \\\\
    1.09861231 & NaN
\end{bmatrix}

$$


$$
Log(B) = \begin{bmatrix}
    0.6931472 &  1.38629436\\\\
    1.79175949 & 2.07944155
\end{bmatrix}

$$

### Log(Vector) = Vector


$$
Log(C) = \begin{bmatrix}
    NaN \\\\
    1.09861231
\end{bmatrix}

$$


$$
Log(D) = \begin{bmatrix}
    NaN & 1.09861231
\end{bmatrix}

$$

### Log(Scalar) = Scalar


$$
Log(1) = 0

$$
