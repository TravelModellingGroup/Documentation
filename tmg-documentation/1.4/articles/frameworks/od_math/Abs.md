# Abs

This applies the absolute function to a single parameter either a scalar, vector, or matrix.

$$

A = \begin{bmatrix}
    -1 & 2          \\\\
     3 & -4
\end{bmatrix}, 
B = \begin{bmatrix}
   2 & 4          \\\\
    6 & 8
\end{bmatrix}, 
C = \begin{bmatrix}
     -1 \\\\
      3
\end{bmatrix}, 
D = \begin{bmatrix}
   -1 & 3
\end{bmatrix} 
$$

## Abs(Scalar) = Scalar


$$
Abs(-1) = 1

$$


$$
Abs(1) = 1

$$

## Abs(Vector) = Vector

The orientation of the vector will be maintained in the resulting vector.


$$
Abs(C) = \begin{bmatrix}
    1 & 3
\end{bmatrix} 
$$

## Abs(Matrix) = Matrix


$$
Abs(A) = \begin{bmatrix}
    1 & 2          \\\\
    3 & 4
\end{bmatrix} 
$$
