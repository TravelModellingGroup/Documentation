# Sum

Returns the summation of all elements in the structure.


$$
A = \begin{bmatrix}
    -1 & 2          \\\\
     3 & -4
\end{bmatrix}, \
B = \begin{bmatrix}
    2 & 4          \\\\
    6 & 8
\end{bmatrix}, 
C = \begin{bmatrix}
    -1 \\\\
     3
\end{bmatrix}, 
D = \begin{bmatrix}
    -1 & 3
\end{bmatrix}

$$

### Sum(Matrix) = Scalar


$$
Sum(A) = 0

$$


$$
Sum(B) = 20

$$

### Sum(Vector) = Scalar


$$
Sum(C) = 2

$$


$$
Sum(D) = 2

$$

### Sum(Scalar) = ERROR


$$
Sum(0) = ERROR

$$
